
Questions and Answers
What was the name of the girl in the story?
Answer: Maya.
What was the name of Maya's dog?
Answer: Rocky.
What did Maya pack before leaving home?
Answer: Food, water, a notebook, a compass, and a flashlight.
What message was written on the old stone bridge?
Answer: "Only those who help others will find the true path."
Which animal did Maya rescue?
Answer: A small fox.
Who did Maya help cross the river?
Answer: An elderly traveler.
What gift did the traveler give Maya?
Answer: A small wooden whistle.
Where did Maya find the ancient map?
Answer: Inside a cave.
What was hidden behind the waterfall?
Answer: A secret passage leading to the Hidden Valley of Silver Springs.
Why was Maya allowed to enter the hidden valley?
Answer: Because she showed kindness, courage, and selflessness.
What did the forest guardian give Maya?
Answer: A tiny silver seed.
What happened after the villagers planted the silver seed?
Answer: It grew into a magnificent tree that protected the village and inspired people to care for nature and one another.
What is the main lesson of the story?
Answer: Kindness, helping others, and caring for nature bring the greatest rewards.
Do you think Maya became a good role model? Why?
Answer: Yes. She was brave, compassionate, and always chose to help others, inspiring her entire village.
